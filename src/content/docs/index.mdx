## What HubMessage does

HubMessage reads your local iMessage history, matches the participants against
contacts in your HubSpot portal, and logs each message as a communication record
on the matching contact. Optionally, it streams those messages into a dedicated
**iMessage channel** in your HubSpot inbox so your team can reply from inside
HubSpot.

It runs as a menu bar app on your Mac. No dock icon, no analytics, no telemetry.
